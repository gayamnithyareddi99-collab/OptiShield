How OptiShieldTech Works

OptiShieldTech is a retrofit-first AI optimisation platform that integrates with existing Building Management Systems (BMS) to deliver safe, measurable, and scalable energy optimisation. The system follows a phased, trust-based deployment model to ensure operational stability, transparency, and verifiable performance improvements across building portfolios.

01

BMS Integration & Data Foundation

OptiShieldTech integrates seamlessly with existing BMS infrastructure, including BACnet and Modbus systems, without requiring system replacement. The platform ingests real-time telemetry such as HVAC signals, environmental conditions, and equipment states, validating and structuring this data to create a reliable optimisation foundation.

02

Observe Mode

In Observe Mode, OptiShieldTech passively analyses historical and live building data to establish baselines for energy consumption, comfort conditions, and equipment behaviour. No control actions are executed, ensuring zero operational risk while building system understanding.

03

Shadow Mode

OptiShieldTech generates optimisation recommendations with quantified energy savings, risk probabilities, and performance impact. These recommendations are reviewed by facility teams before execution, ensuring transparency, auditability, and operational trust.

04

Guarded Control

Approved optimisation actions are deployed through controlled write-backs to the BMS with strict safety constraints, rollback protection, and continuous monitoring. OptiShieldTech ensures energy savings while maintaining comfort, compliance, and equipment integrity at all times.

OptiShieldTech Platform

OptiShieldTech is a retrofit-first, AI-driven optimisation layer that integrates with existing Building Management Systems (BMS) to safely reduce energy consumption, lower carbon emissions, and improve operational efficiency without requiring system replacement or complex retrofitting.

BMS Integration Layer

Seamless integration with existing systems.

OptiShieldTech connects directly to existing BMS infrastructure using standard protocols such as BACnet and Modbus. It enables fast deployment without hardware upgrades, custom engineering, or system redesign.

Observe → Shadow → Guarded Control

Phased, trust-based deployment model.

The platform follows a staged rollout: Observe Mode establishes baselines, Shadow Mode generates optimisation recommendations with risk insights, and Guarded Control safely executes approved actions with full monitoring and rollback protection.

AI Optimisation Engine

Learns and adapts to building behaviour.

OptiShieldTech analyses real-time and historical telemetry to identify patterns and optimise system performance dynamically, reducing reliance on static rules or manual configuration.

Safety Constraint Engine

Non-negotiable safety and comfort limits.

All optimisation actions are validated against strict constraints such as temperature, humidity, CO₂ levels, and equipment limits, ensuring compliance, occupant comfort, and equipment protection at all times.

Measurement & Verification (M&V)

Transparent, audit-ready savings.

OptiShieldTech delivers Measurement & Verification by comparing performance against baseline conditions, providing clear, data-driven energy savings insights for reporting and validation.

Execution & Rollback Control

Safe automation with full control.

Optimisation actions are deployed with continuous monitoring, anomaly detection, and deterministic rollback mechanisms, ensuring systems can safely revert to baseline operation when required.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn how OptiShieldTech helps commercial buildings reduce energy waste, improve operational efficiency, and safely optimise existing Building Management Systems.

What is OptiShieldTech?

OptiShieldTech is a retrofit-first, AI-driven optimisation layer that works on top of existing Building Management Systems (BMS). It enables buildings to reduce energy consumption, lower carbon emissions, and improve operational efficiency without replacing existing infrastructure.

How does OptiShieldTech reduce energy consumption?

The platform analyses live building data such as HVAC signals, occupancy, and environmental conditions. It applies optimisation strategies like schedule trimming, temperature resets, and system balancing to reduce unnecessary energy usage while maintaining comfort and performance.

Does OptiShieldTech require hardware upgrades?

No. OptiShieldTech is designed as a software layer that integrates directly with existing BMS systems such as BACnet and Modbus. It avoids costly hardware replacement and reduces engineering effort during deployment.

How does OptiShieldTech ensure safety and comfort?

OptiShieldTech operates within strict safety constraints including temperature, humidity, and equipment limits. All optimisation actions are validated before execution, and unsafe actions are automatically blocked or adjusted to ensure comfort, compliance, and equipment protection.

What are Observe, Shadow, and Guarded Control modes?

The platform deploys in phases. Observe Mode builds baseline understanding from live data, Shadow Mode generates optimisation recommendations with expected impact, and Guarded Control Mode safely executes approved actions with rollback protection and continuous monitoring.

How are energy savings measured?

OptiShieldTech uses loop-level Measurement & Verification (M&V) to quantify savings. Energy improvements are normalised for weather and occupancy, ensuring results are transparent, auditable, and aligned with real operational performance.

Who should use OptiShieldTech?

OptiShieldTech is designed for commercial buildings, property portfolios, and facilities teams seeking to reduce energy costs, improve sustainability, and enhance building performance without complex upgrades or engineering-heavy projects.
